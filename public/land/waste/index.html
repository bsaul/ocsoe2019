The following interactive graphic shows tonnage of Municipal Solid Waste + Construction and Demoltion Waste and Recycling for all NC counties according to the 2016-17 NC DEQ annual reports (2017). In this year, Orange County reported 0.52 tons of waste per person and 0.16 tons of reycling per person. Recycling accounted for 24% of all reported waste and recycling.
